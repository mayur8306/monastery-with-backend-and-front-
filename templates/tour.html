Virtual Monastery Tours

Step inside the serene and sacred monasteries of Sikkim from anywhere in the world.

Rumtek Monastery

Also known as the Dharmachakra Centre, Rumtek is a vibrant center for Tibetan Buddhism with ornate prayer halls and stunning courtyards.

Pemayangtse Monastery

One of the oldest in Sikkim, Pemayangtse is famous for its architecture and annual religious festivals.

Tashiding Monastery

Perched on a heart-shaped hill, Tashiding is considered the spiritual center of Sikkim with breathtaking views of the surrounding valley.

Ralang Monastery

Ralang is famous for its thangkas and hosts the Pang Lhabsol festival with vibrant mask dances.

Enchey Monastery

Located near Gangtok, Enchey Monastery is a serene place known for its vibrant architecture and religious rituals.

Lingdum (Ranka) Monastery

Ranka Monastery is surrounded by forested hills and serves as a training center for young lamas.

Phodong Monastery

Phodong is a large North Sikkim monastery, famous for its festivals and historical significance.

Dubdi Monastery

Dubdi, founded in 1701, is Sikkim's oldest monastery, known for its serene atmosphere and historical importance.

Sanga Choeling Monastery

Sanga Choeling is a small but historically important monastery, perched on a hilltop overlooking the Teesta River.

Karma Dubgyu Chholing Monastery

A peaceful monastery located in West Sikkim, known for its meditation sessions and scenic surroundings.
